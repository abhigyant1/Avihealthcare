We have the best service of Ambulance

Ambulance services are the primary providers of a 24/7 response to medical and trauma related emergencies. We provide a disciplined and organised system, allowing a timely response of appropriately qualified health care workers – often to potential or confirmed medical emergencies. Although medical retrieval teams are provided by the major trauma centres, coordination of the team and rescue helicopter is provided by the ambulance communications centre, and also crewed in most instances with intensive care paramedics.
